Overall Hygiene

Hygiene is an extremely important aspect of any food business, but it is especially critical when it comes to street food carts. At New Gen Thela, we prioritize hygiene and take great care to maintain cleanliness in our food carts. We regularly clean and sanitize all surfaces and equipment and train our staff on proper food handling and safety practices.

Regular Quality Checks

. At New Gen Thela, we understand the importance of maintaining consistent quality in all aspects of our business, Our "Lumberjack" i.e our quality spy conducts regular quality checks to ensure high standards and provide assistance to the owner. Our focus on hygiene, customer interaction, and marketing sets us apart in the traditional Indian street food scene.

Franchise Model

New Gen Thela is excited to offer a franchise opportunity for individuals or businesses who are interested in joining our growing network of modernized street food carts. Our franchise model is designed to provide the necessary support and resources to help franchisees succeed, including initial training, ongoing support, and access to our proven systems and processes.

Modern Marketing Techniques

Professional Behaviour

At New Gen Thela, we believe that professional behavior is essential for delivering the best possible experience to our customers.
