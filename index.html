tasty Masala Dosa

Restaurant style crispy masala dosa, Aromatic and delicious made from rice, lentils, potato, fenugreek, ghee and curry leaves, and served with chutneys and sambar. Typical ingredients include rice, husked black gram, puffed rice, chana dal, fenugreek seeds, salt, ghee, potatoes, onion, green chillies, curry leaves, and turmeric

tasty Cheese Dosa

Chesse dosa is one of our most loved dishes, made with regular batter topped with cheese. It is an irresistible treat of crisp and butter dosa layered with a loads of cheese.
To make it spicy you can also smear some chatni, curry or any sauce and then top with cheese
